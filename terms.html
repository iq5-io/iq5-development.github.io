  • Storage Optimization: Analysis of storage costs and inventory turnover rates

  • Campaign Performance: Comprehensive analysis of advertising campaign effectiveness
  • ROI Optimization: Budget allocation recommendations based on performance data
  • Keyword Performance: Monitoring and optimization of advertising keywords
  • ACOS Management: Advertising Cost of Sale analysis and optimization

  • Sales Analytics: Detailed performance reporting and revenue analysis
  • Growth Insights: Identification of growth opportunities and market trends
  • Performance Benchmarking: Comparison against category performance metrics
  • Forecasting Models: AI-powered sales and demand forecasting
